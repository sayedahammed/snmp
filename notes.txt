Notes
_____________

.1.3.6.1.4.1.8072.1.3.2.2.1.3.6.109.121.110.97.109.101

This OID corresponds to:

NET-SNMP-EXTEND-MIB::nsExtendConfigTable.nsExtendConfigEntry.nsExtendConfigCommand."myname"

What’s going on?

.1.3.6.1.4.1.8072 is the Net-SNMP enterprise branch.

.1.3.6.1.4.1.8072.1.3 is the nsExtend subtree.

The part 2.2.1.3 maps to nsExtendConfigCommand — the command the extend runs.

The trailing 6.109.121.110.97.109.101 is the string "myname" in length+ASCII.
